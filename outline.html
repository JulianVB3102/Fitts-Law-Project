Fitts' Law Experiment
4 diameters × 4 distances × 2 directions × 10 blocks = 320 trials

Welcome

This study measures your pointing performance by asking you to move from a center "home" to click a target circle as quickly and accurately as possible.

You must be 18+ to participate. Data recorded: timing, cursor path length, hits/misses, and task parameters. No personal identifiers are collected in this study.

  1. Place the cursor over the center Home dot.
  2. Press the Home dot to start a trial. A target circle will appear either left or right.
  3. Move to the target and click it as fast and accurately as you can.
  4. If you miss (click outside), it still counts and the next trial will begin.
  5. Complete all trials. You can pause between blocks if needed.

Tips: Use the same seating and device position for consistency. Avoid screen zoom changes during the test.
